Analytics Project 2: Customer Analytics & Insights

Comprehensive customer analytics platform analyzing behavior, churn prediction, and lifetime value optimization.

Analysis Type

Cohort Analysis, Churn Prediction, LTV Calculation

Tools & Languages

Python, R, SQL, Jupyter Notebooks, Matplotlib/Seaborn

Dataset

500K+ customers | 3 years of historical data | 50+ features

Key Finding

Identified segments with 60% churn risk | Actionable retention strategies

Project Walkthrough

Step 1: Data Collection & Preparation

Aggregated customer data from multiple sources. Created customer 360 views combining behavioral, transactional, and demographic data.

Step 2: Exploratory Analysis

Analyzed customer journey, lifecycle stages, and churn patterns. Identified key drivers of churn through correlation and statistical tests.

Step 3: Cohort & Segmentation Analysis

Performed cohort analysis to track customer behavior over time. Segmented customers by acquisition cohort, value, and risk level.

Built churn prediction models and LTV forecasting models. Identified high-risk and high-value customer segments for targeted campaigns.

Step 5: Recommendations & Implementation

Provided data-driven recommendations for retention strategies. Monitored campaign effectiveness and refined approaches based on results.
